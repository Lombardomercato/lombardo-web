# LOMBARDO — Project Context

Version: 1.0

This document explains the business context and strategic goals of the Lombardo website.

AI agents must read this file before making design or development decisions.

---

# 1. About the Business

Lombardo is a boutique wine bar and specialty coffee space located in Pueblo Esther, near Rosario, Argentina.

The concept combines:

wine culture  
specialty coffee  
simple gastronomy  
social experiences  

The place is designed to be a warm and aesthetically curated meeting point.

---

# 2. Business Identity

Lombardo is positioned as:

a boutique destination  
a lifestyle experience  
a social meeting space  

It should feel more like a **wine bar experience** than a traditional retail wine store.

Atmosphere is extremely important.

---

# 3. Target Audience

Primary audience:

Adults between 25 and 55.

Profile:

people who appreciate aesthetics  
wine lovers  
specialty coffee enthusiasts  
couples and groups of friends  
middle to upper-middle income  

Many visitors live in residential areas near Rosario or have weekend houses nearby.

---

# 4. Brand Positioning

Lombardo is not:

a cheap wine store  
a corporate café  
a traditional bar  

Lombardo is:

a curated experience  
a stylish but relaxed place  
a modern wine bar with personality.

---

# 5. Website Purpose

The website should:

introduce the Lombardo concept  
show the atmosphere  
create desire to visit  
present wine and coffee experiences  
build brand identity

The website is primarily a **brand experience website**, not an e-commerce platform.

---

# 6. Desired Visitor Actions

The main actions visitors should take:

visit Lombardo in person  
follow Lombardo on social media  
contact Lombardo  
attend events or tastings  

Secondary future actions:

join wine club  
book private events  
buy wine online

---

# 7. Competitive Inspiration

The digital experience should feel closer to:

boutique restaurants  
Italian wine bars  
modern lifestyle cafés

rather than retail websites.

---

# 8. Emotional Goal

When visitors see the website, they should think:

"This place looks beautiful."
"I want to go there."
"This feels special."

The website must create emotional attraction.

---

# 9. Visual Direction

Visual emphasis should be on:

atmosphere  
photography  
typography  
minimal layouts  

Information density should remain low.

---

# 10. Strategic Priorities

The website must prioritize:

brand identity  
visual experience  
emotional impact  
clarity  

More important than showing many details is creating a strong and memorable impression.

---

# 11. Long Term Vision

In the future Lombardo may include:

wine club memberships  
online wine sales  
event reservations  
private experiences

The website architecture should allow expansion later.
