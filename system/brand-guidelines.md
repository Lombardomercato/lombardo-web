# LOMBARDO — Brand Guidelines

Version: 2.0
Brand: Lombardo
Business type: Mercato de encuentro / café / vino / experiencias
Location: Pueblo Esther, Santa Fe, Argentina

This document defines the official brand identity for the Lombardo website and digital ecosystem.

- design-system.md
- website-structure.md

# 1. Brand Essence

Lombardo is a meeting point in Pueblo Esther that combines tradition and modernity to offer an authentic, accessible and quality gastronomic experience.

It is not just a café.
It is not just a wine shop.
It is not just a bar.

Lombardo is:
- a social meeting point
- a curated local destination
- a place for coffee, wine and good moments
- a boutique but accessible concept
- a brand with neighborhood roots and aesthetic identity

---

# 2. Core Concept

Main brand idea:

TU PUNTO DE ENCUENTRO EN PUEBLO ESTHER.

Secondary claim:

MERCATO DE ENCUENTRO

Additional brand expression used in communication:

CAFÉ, VINO Y BUENOS MOMENTOS.

The website should communicate Lombardo as a place where people meet, stay, share and return.

---

# 3. Mission, Vision and Values

## Mission
Brindar un servicio que inspire y genere momentos memorables, sin perder la esencia del barrio y la cultura argentina.

## Vision
Construir una marca que trascienda, evoque historia y pertenencia, y se convierta en un ícono dentro del sector gastronómico.

## Brand values
- Cercanía
- Comunidad
- Diferenciación

These values must appear in the visual tone and in the website copy.

---

# 4. Brand Personality

Lombardo must feel:

- warm
- welcoming
- authentic
- stylish
- accessible
- modern with roots
- social
- memorable

It should never feel:

- cold
- corporate
- luxury-snob
- generic
- tech-startup-like
- overloaded with visual effects

---

# 5. Brand Archetype

Main archetype:
Compañero

Secondary archetype:
Explorador

How this should be translated digitally:
- clear and welcoming communication
- sense of belonging
- friendly guidance
- discovery without elitism
- curated experiences without pretension

---

# 6. Voice and Tone

## Voice
The voice is:
- authentic
- close
- clear
- friendly
- Argentine in spirit, but never forced
- with personality, without pretension

Lombardo speaks like someone who knows about good food and drink, but does not lecture.

## Tone by context

### Website brand sections
Warm, emotional, inviting.

### Menu and product information
Clear, simple, appetizing, direct.

### Events and club
Social, attractive, with identity.

### Contact and service
Friendly, practical, easy.

Avoid:
- corporate marketing language
- exaggerated luxury claims
- technical jargon
- empty phrases

---

# 7. Target Audience

## Primary audience
- men and women roughly 25 to 55
- middle and upper-middle segment
- people looking for quality and something different
- residents of Pueblo Esther and nearby areas
- weekend visitors
- couples, groups of friends, families
- people who value aesthetics, coffee, wine and shared moments

## Secondary audience
- wholesale / gastronomic business clients in the region
- gift buyers
- people interested in delicatessen and curated products

# 8. Official Color Palette

Only use the official brand colors.

## Azul principal
HEX: #003A70

## Verde claro
HEX: #D4EB8E

## Coral
HEX: #FFB3AB

## Gris rosado
HEX: #E4D5D3

## Rojo Lombardo
HEX: #E03C31

Color rules:
- Do not introduce additional colors outside this palette.
- The website should rely mainly on Azul principal, Gris rosado, Rojo Lombardo and Coral.
- Verde claro is a support accent, never the dominant color.
- Contrast and readability must always be preserved.
- Color combinations must balance elegance and freshness.

---

# 9. Typography

## Primary typeface
Gopher

Allowed weights:
- Light
- Regular
- Medium
- Bold

Usage:
- headings
- subheadings
- short phrases
- statements
- hero text
- featured callouts

Mandatory rules for Gopher:
- ALWAYS UPPERCASE
- never use in long paragraphs
- never use in lowercase
- keep it for display text only
- use it to reinforce identity and memorability

## Secondary typeface
Articulat CF

Allowed weights:
- Normal
- Demi Bold
- Bold

Usage:
- paragraphs
- descriptions
- navigation
- UI text
- digital menu
- forms
- long text
- practical information

---

# 10. Logo Rules

The logo is the most important brand asset.

Rules:
- keep the official proportions
- keep the required safety space around it
- do not rotate it
- do not distort it
- do not add gradients
- do not add shadows
- do not change colors outside approved applications
- choose the best logo version according to contrast and background

Digital minimum width:
170 px

---

# 11. Photography and Graphic Style

Photography should communicate:
- real moments
- coffee
- wine
- products
- shared tables
- bottles
- warmth
- neighborhood atmosphere with aesthetic curation

Avoid:
- cold stock photography
- overly staged corporate images
- noisy compositions
- futuristic visual language

Graphic resources may include:
- abstract illustrations related to products
- subtle graphic shapes from the identity
- editorial image layouts
- large brand color planes
