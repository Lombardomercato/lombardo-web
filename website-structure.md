# LOMBARDO — Website Structure

Version: 1.0

This document defines the website architecture, page structure, content hierarchy, and UX priorities for the Lombardo website.

It must be used together with:
- brand-guidelines.md
- design-system.md

Main goal:
Create a website that feels elegant, warm, minimal and premium, reflecting the identity of Lombardo as a wine bar, specialty coffee space, and social meeting place.

---

# 1. Website Objective

The website should:

- present Lombardo as a boutique and aspirational destination
- communicate the concept clearly: wine + specialty coffee + experiences
- generate desire to visit
- showcase the atmosphere and identity of the brand
- make it easy to contact, locate, and follow the brand
- be visually strong, modern and emotionally engaging

This is not a corporate website.
This is a lifestyle / hospitality website.

---

# 2. Website Type

Recommended website type:

Single-page landing website with strong storytelling sections.

Optional future expansion:
- menu page
- events page
- reservations page
- wine club page
- online shop page

Initial priority:
Create a premium and complete landing page.

---

# 3. Main Navigation

Main menu items:

- Inicio
- Café
- Vinos
- Experiencias
- Galería
- Contacto

Optional menu items for future versions:
- Carta
- Eventos
- Club de vinos
- Reservas

Navigation style:
simple, elegant, sticky, minimal

---

# 4. Homepage Structure

The homepage should contain the following sections in this exact order:

1. Hero
2. Brand Intro / Concept
3. Specialty Coffee
4. Wine Experience
5. Experiences and Events
6. Gallery / Atmosphere
7. Visit Us / Contact
8. Footer

Each section must feel spacious, editorial and visually refined.

---

# 5. Section Details

## 5.1 Hero Section

Purpose:
Create an emotional first impression and introduce the brand immediately.

Content:
- Logo or brand name
- Short headline
- Supporting phrase
- Main call to action
- Optional secondary call to action
- Strong background image or elegant visual composition

Suggested headline ideas:
- LOMBARDO
- vino · café · encuentros
- un lugar para disfrutar sin apuro

Suggested supporting copy:
Boutique wine bar and specialty coffee in a warm, elegant and social atmosphere.

CTA options:
- Ver experiencia
- Descubrir Lombardo
- Contactanos

Visual direction:
The hero must feel premium, cinematic, warm, and minimal.

---

## 5.2 Brand Intro / Concept

Purpose:
Explain what Lombardo is and what makes it special.

Content:
- Short section title
- 1 or 2 paragraphs about the concept
- Optional image
- Optional highlight phrases

Core message:
Lombardo is not just a wine bar.
It is a place where wine, coffee, aesthetics and shared moments come together.

Suggested section title:
- El espíritu de Lombardo
- Un lugar con identidad
- Más que una vinería

Suggested text themes:
- boutique wine bar
- specialty coffee
- social and warm atmosphere
- modern Italian inspiration
- premium but approachable experience

---

## 5.3 Specialty Coffee Section

Purpose:
Show that coffee is an important part of the experience, not a secondary detail.

Content:
- Section title
- Short descriptive paragraph
- Visual support
- Optional list of featured offerings

Suggested title:
- Café de especialidad
- El ritual del café
- Buen café, sin apuro

Suggested supporting text:
Lombardo also celebrates specialty coffee with a relaxed, refined and inviting approach.

Possible featured items:
- espresso
- flat white
- cappuccino
- latte
- coffee with pastries
- breakfast and afternoon options

Design note:
This section should feel calm, warm and elegant.

---

## 5.4 Wine Experience Section

Purpose:
Make wine the emotional and visual center of the brand.

Content:
- Section title
- Supporting paragraph
- Optional cards or highlights
- Optional featured experiences
- Optional wine-by-the-glass positioning

Suggested title:
- Cultura del vino
- La experiencia del vino
- Una selección para disfrutar

Suggested themes:
- curated wine selection
- wines by the glass
- boutique labels
- discovery and enjoyment
- no pressure, no snobbery

Possible highlights:
- selección cuidada
- vinos por copa
- etiquetas boutique
- recomendaciones personalizadas

Design note:
This section should feel rich, elegant, and sensorial.

---

## 5.5 Experiences and Events Section

Purpose:
Show Lombardo as a social place where memorable moments happen.

Content:
- Section title
- Paragraph explaining the experience
- Optional event cards
- Optional icons or short bullets

Suggested title:
- Experiencias Lombardo
- Catas, encuentros y momentos
- Eventos con identidad

Suggested themes:
- wine tastings
- private events
- after office moments
- balcony gatherings
- special nights
- social experiences with aesthetic value

Possible content blocks:
- Catas guiadas
- Eventos privados
- Encuentros en el balcón
- Noches especiales
- Happy hour

Design note:
This section should feel social, inviting and elevated.

---

## 5.6 Gallery / Atmosphere Section

Purpose:
Sell the mood and visual identity of the place.

Content:
- Section title
- Minimal intro sentence
- Curated gallery
- Images of wine, coffee, glasses, space, textures, people, lighting

Suggested title:
- La atmósfera
- Un lugar para quedarse
- Lombardo en imágenes

Image direction:
- warm tones
- elegant shadows
- real moments
- bottles, cups, tables, textures, balcony, gatherings

Design note:
This section should be highly visual and emotional.

---

## 5.7 Visit Us / Contact Section

Purpose:
Make it easy for visitors to take action.

Content:
- Address
- City / area
- Opening hours
- Social media
- WhatsApp or contact button
- Optional map embed
- Optional short invitation text

Suggested title:
- Visitanos
- Encontranos
- Te esperamos en Lombardo

Suggested closing text:
Come for a glass, stay for the moment.

CTA options:
- Escribinos
- Ver ubicación
- Reservar experiencia

Important:
This section must be clear, practical, and easy to use.

---

## 5.8 Footer

Purpose:
Close the site with elegance and useful information.

Footer should contain:
- Lombardo logo or wordmark
- Short brand phrase
- Navigation links
- Contact info
- Social media
- Opening hours

Suggested phrase:
vino · café · encuentros

Style:
dark, elegant, simple

---

# 6. Content Tone

The website copy should feel:

- warm
- refined
- human
- relaxed
- elegant

Avoid:
- corporate tone
- generic marketing phrases
- overly formal language
- exaggerated luxury language

The tone should feel like:
a tasteful host inviting people into a beautiful experience.

---

# 7. Calls to Action

Use few but meaningful CTAs.

Recommended CTA texts:
- Descubrir Lombardo
- Ver experiencia
- Contactanos
- Visitanos
- Escribinos
- Reservar experiencia

Avoid aggressive sales language.

---

# 8. Visual Priorities

The design should prioritize:

1. atmosphere
2. typography
3. spacing
4. photography
5. emotional clarity

This website should not feel overloaded with information.
It should feel curated.

---

# 9. Mobile Priorities

On mobile:
- simplify layout
- keep strong typography
- ensure spacing remains premium
- stack sections clearly
- keep navigation simple
- prioritize readability and touch targets

The mobile version must feel as elegant as desktop.

---

If the site expands later, recommended pages are:

## 10.1 Carta

Show food, pastries, coffee, wine by the glass, drinks and combos.

## 10.2 Eventos
Purpose:
Show upcoming tastings, special evenings, private events and social experiences.

## 10.3 Club de vinos
Purpose:
Explain membership plans, benefits and subscription options.

## 10.4 Reservas
Purpose:
Allow booking for tastings, events or private gatherings.

## 10.5 Tienda online
Purpose:
Sell wines, gifts, gourmet products and future branded items.

---

# 11. SEO / Semantic Recommendations

Use semantic sections and clear headings.

Recommended homepage content hierarchy:
- One H1 only
- Clear H2 sections
- Descriptive alt text on images
- Strong local signals: Pueblo Esther / Rosario / Santa Fe
- Brand-based metadata
- Warm, human copy

Suggested SEO themes:
- vinería boutique
- wine bar
- café de especialidad
- Pueblo Esther
- Rosario
- experiencias de vino
- catas

---

# 12. Final User Experience Goal

When someone visits the website, they should immediately feel:

- this place has identity
- this place is aesthetically curated
- this place is warm and memorable
- this place is worth visiting

The website must create desire, trust and emotional connection.

Final perception:
Lombardo is a modern, elegant and welcoming destination where wine, coffee and good moments come together.
